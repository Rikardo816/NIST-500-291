Cloud Computing Standards for Accessibility

Relevance of Accessibility

Accessibility is relevant to cloud computing services at the application level where human interaction occurs. This is where accessibility is measured and existing ICT application accessibility standards apply.

U.S. Access Board

An independent federal agency devoted to accessibility for people with disabilities. Its responsibilities include:

  • Developing design criteria for the built environment
  • Setting standards for transit vehicles and telecommunications equipment
  • Providing technical assistance and training
  • Enforcing accessibility standards for federally funded facilities

Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act

This law requires that:

  • Federal employees with disabilities have comparable access to information
  • Members of the public with disabilities have comparable access to federal agency services

Applicable standards for many cloud computing applications:

  • 1194.21 Software applications and operating systems
  • 1194.22 Web-based intranet and internet information and applications
  • 1194.23 Telecommunications products

Additional Standards

  • W3C's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0
  • ISO 9241-20:2008 and ISO 9241-171:2008
  • ANSI/HFES 200 Human Factors Engineering of Software User Interfaces
  • ISO/IEC 24751-1:2008
